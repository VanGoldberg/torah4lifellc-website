About Our Founder

Torah 4 Life LLC was founded by Darleen Regina VanGoldberg to illuminate the path of Torah-centered living in a world increasingly disconnected from sacred truth. Through divine guidance, covenant conviction, and a passion for restoration, this work serves as both a witness and a resource to those returning to YAH’s eternal ways. With deep reverence for the commandments, festivals, and promises of the Most High, this platform exists to uplift, teach, and equip seekers to walk in truth, holiness, and righteousness.
